Key functions

Movement

  • Press W A S D to move around.
  • Press Space to jump.
  • Press Q to dash & hold W to control it with your mouse.
  • Hold A or D and press Q to side dash, control with your mouse beware its very fast so it needs experience to master.
  • Hold S and press Q to Backdash, control it with your mouse.

    • Skills

  • Press 1,2,3 or 4 to use your skills based on your character.
